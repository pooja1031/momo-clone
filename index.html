The WOW! Momo Story

Started by classmates Sagar Daryani and Binod Homagai, Kolkata based
company Wow! Momo was started on 29th August 2008. Hailing from St
Xavier's Kolkata in their final year of their graduation they took the final plunge
and decided to give consumers an experience and not just Momo! So simply
they called it Wow! Momo. The basic idea was to create an experience that
would leave their customers with only one word and that is WOW!
If you have grown up in Kolkata - you ought to be a foodie
and if you are a foodie you would know the fellow foodie's centricity and preference.

Some of our wide range of products with which have experimented include
Cheese Momos, Augratin, Sizzlers, Butter Masala Momo, Darjeeling Momo
The Moburg was a big innovation that rightly combined Moros with Burgers and
was a lip-smacking meal for everyone. The journey from just playing with Momos
to introducing so man new dishes has been challenging and excruciating but a
roller coaster ride.
Today Wow! Moro has over 300 outlets across 17 cities and the hunger
and passion for what they do still lingers!
